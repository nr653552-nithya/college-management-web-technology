❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for admission? +
You can apply online through our admissions portal. Visit the Admissions page, fill the application form, upload required documents, and pay the application fee online.
What is the counselling code? +
Our counselling code is 1414. You need to enter this code during TNEA counselling for engineering admissions.
Is there hostel facility available? +
Yes, we have separate boys and girls hostels with modern amenities, 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, and mess facilities.
What is the placement record? +
We have an excellent placement record with 95% placement rate. Top recruiters include Google, Microsoft, Amazon, TCS, Infosys, and many more.
Are scholarships available? +
Yes, we offer merit scholarships, sports quota scholarships, government scholarships for SC/ST/OBC, and defence quota scholarships.
How can I contact the placement cell? +
You can contact placement cell at +91 98765 43212 or email placement@prince.edu. Office hours: Monday-Friday 9 AM to 5 PM.
