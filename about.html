Our Glorious History

Established in 1995, Trinity - Institute of Engineering and Technology has been a beacon of quality education in Chennai. What started as a modest institution with 3 courses and 200 students has now grown into one of Tamil Nadu's premier engineering colleges.

Over the past three decades, we have consistently pushed the boundaries of academic excellence, innovation, and research. Our journey from a local college to a nationally recognized institution reflects our unwavering commitment to student success.

Today, we stand proud as an institution that has produced over 15,000+ alumni working in top companies across the globe, from Google and Microsoft to leading startups and research organizations.

Our Vision
