Welcome to Our Green Campus

Spread across 50+ acres of lush greenery, TRINITY campus offers a perfect blend of academic excellence and vibrant student life. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern amenities, and supportive environment ensure holistic development of every student.

🏆 World-Class Facilities

📚 Central Library

50,000+ books, 200+ journals, digital library with 24/7 access, e-resources, and research databases.

🔬 Modern Labs

35+ specialized labs with cutting-edge equipment for hands-on learning and research.

🏋️ Sports Complex

Indoor stadium, cricket ground, football field, basketball courts, gymnasium, and swimming pool.

💻 Smart Classrooms

AC classrooms with smart boards, projectors, and high-speed Wi-Fi connectivity.
